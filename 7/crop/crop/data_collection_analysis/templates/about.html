About OptiCrop

OptiCrop is an intelligent agricultural recommendation system that leverages machine learning and data science to help farmers make informed decisions about crop selection. Our system analyzes environmental factors, soil properties, and historical crop data to provide personalized crop recommendations.

Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers with data-driven insights that optimize agricultural production, improve resource efficiency, and promote sustainable farming practices. By providing intelligent crop recommendations, OptiCrop helps farmers maximize their yields and minimize resource wastage.

How It Works

OptiCrop uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on over 2,200 crop samples to analyze your soil and environmental conditions. Our model considers seven critical factors:

Nitrogen (N) Essential for plant growth, leaf development, and protein synthesis.
Phosphorus (P) Critical for root development, flowering, seeding, and energy transfer.
Potassium (K) Improves plant strength, water regulation, and disease resistance.
Temperature Regulates enzymatic activities and affects crop growth rate and maturity.
Humidity Influences crop transpiration, disease susceptibility, and photosynthesis.
pH Level Determines nutrient availability and biological activities in the soil.
Rainfall Primary water source determining cell turgidity and overall growth.
